Labour Insights Project

Policy and insights

This project translated Stonehaven’s political MRP and voter segmentation analysis into clear visual narratives following the 2024 General Election.

Working closely with data scientists and political consultants, data visualisation shaped how complex modelling outputs were communicated to business audiences, linking quantitative insight with policy expertise to underline the dynamics shaping the new political landscape.

Politics of the road

TRANSPORT, POLICY AND INSIGHTS

This project translated polling and economic analysis on motoring tax into a clear visual framework, supporting Stonehaven’s work on how the system is evolving as electric vehicles become more widespread.

The visual work focused on bringing differences in tax treatment across generations and vehicle types into focus, structuring complex trade-offs to support a clear argument: that early, considered reform may be less disruptive than delaying change.
