Project Tempo Visual Storytelling & Communications

Data visualisation and storytelling

Alongside analytical tools and platforms, I support Project Tempo's day-to-day communications through the design of data-driven visuals for reports, social media and thought leadership. Working closely with insights and digital teams, I translate complex polling and modelling outputs into clear, client-facing graphics that help evidence-based messages land across a wide range of audiences.

A central focus of this work is respecting and reinforcing the client’s established voice and visual identity while ensuring analytical integrity and clarity. This involves shaping narratives around key findings, simplifying technical material without losing substance, and designing visuals that can travel across channels – from editorial content to presentations and social platforms.

In parallel, I support internal capability-building by developing guidance and delivering training on visual best practice, helping teams produce consistent, high-quality outputs in their day-to-day work. Together, this ensures that visualisation is not treated as a one-off deliverable, but as a shared capability that strengthens how insight is communicated over time.
