Project Tempo Europulse Platform

Data product development

EuroPulse is a custom-designed and custom-built analytical platform exploring voter attitudes to the economy, politics and climate across 25 European countries and over 1,000 sub-regions, with advanced modelling extending coverage to more than 12,000 local areas. I led the end-to-end design and development of the platform, translating complex MRP modelling outputs into a usable product and building the application from the ground up â€“ spanning interaction design, front-end development, data integration and deployment.

Developed in close collaboration with data scientists and researchers, EuroPulse combines commissioned survey data with official regional statistics to enable geographically detailed insight beyond standard polling. For 14 countries, richer national models provide finer local resolution while maintaining consistency across the Europe-wide dataset. The platform incorporates both national and European benchmarks, supporting comparison across countries, regions and demographic groups to reflect the international nature of the client's work.

The client's website was originally developed externally. As part of delivery, I collaborated closely with the web development agency to integrate the dashboard into the existing site. I subsequently took responsibility for the full platform, including hosting and maintenance, bringing ongoing ownership in-house to reduce costs and simplify management. The result is a production-grade analytical product supporting both high-level strategy and local decision-making, representing a step-change in Project Tempo's European analytics capability.
